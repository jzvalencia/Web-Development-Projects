The Best Albums According to Jazz

My top 4 albums of all-time

Since I Left you - The Avalanches

Simply perfect album. Experimental yet so catchy, experimental but truly danceable

Soundtracks for the Blind - Swans

A round-view of the human condition thorough painful experiences. A nightmare made music

Leaves Turn Inside you - Unwound

The only bad part is it needed more strings.

Ascension - John Coltrane

Brilliant and beautifully wild. The perfect representation of the free-jazz movement
